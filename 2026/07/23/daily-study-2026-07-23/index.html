今日主线：IELTS Academic · AWL Sublist 1 · 4 个新词。

📖 IELTS Academic 新词（4 个）

本组按 AWL 高频子表推进，并刻意打散相同首字母，避免相似词互相干扰。

🔊 跟读

# Word 音标 核心义 词族
1 authority /ɒ:’θɒriti/ 权威；主管机构 authorities
2 contract /‘kɒntrækt/ 合同；收缩；感染 contracts, contracted, contracting
3 environment /in’vairәnmәnt/ 环境；自然环境 environments
4 respond /ri’spɒnd/ 回应；作出反应 responding, responded, responds

搭配与语境

  • authority · local authorities / have authority
    • Local authorities are responsible for maintaining public transport.
  • contract · sign a contract / contract a disease
    • Employees should read the contract carefully before signing it.
  • environment · protect the environment / learning environment
    • Public transport can reduce pressure on the environment.
  • respond · respond to / respond effectively
    • Schools must respond to the changing needs of learners.

主动回忆

  1. 遮住中文，口头解释每个词。
  2. 从中选 3 个词，各写一句与 IELTS 常见议题有关的句子。
  3. 检查词形和搭配，不追求堆砌生僻词。
展开英文释义
  • authority — a person or organisation with official power or expert knowledge
  • contract — a legal agreement; to become smaller or acquire an illness
  • environment — the natural world or the conditions in which people live and work
  • respond — to answer or react to something
