📖 IELTS Academic 新词（15 个）

本组按 AWL 高频子表推进，并刻意打散相同首字母，避免相似词互相干扰。

🔊 跟读

# Word 音标 核心义 词族

3 available /ә’veilәbl/ 可获得的；可用的 —
4 benefit /‘benifit/ 益处；使受益 benefits, benefited, benefiting
5 concept /‘kɒnsept/ 概念；观念 concepts

搭配与语境

  • analyse · analyse data / analyse the causes
    • Researchers analysed the data to identify the main causes of the decline.
  • approach · a practical approach / adopt an approach
    • Governments should adopt a balanced approach to urban development.
  • available · widely available / available resources
    • Reliable information should be freely available to the public.
  • benefit · bring benefits / benefit from
    • Both employers and staff can benefit from flexible working arrangements.
  • concept · a key concept / the concept of
    • The concept of sustainability now shapes many public policies.
  • data · collect data / reliable data
    • The conclusion is supported by data from several countries.
  • estimate · estimate the cost / a rough estimate
    • Experts estimate that demand will double within twenty years.
  • factor · a key factor / contributing factor
    • Cost is a key factor in students’ choice of accommodation.
  • interpret · interpret data / interpret results
    • The findings should be interpreted with caution.
  • legislate · legislate against / legislate to protect
    • Governments may need to legislate to protect vulnerable workers.
  • method · research method / effective method
    • The researchers used a mixed method to evaluate the programme.
  • occur · occur when / frequently occur
    • Communication problems often occur when responsibilities are unclear.
  • percent · a high percent / increase by ten percent
    • Nearly 60 percent of participants supported the proposal.
  • period · over a period / a short period
    • The population grew rapidly over a twenty-year period.
  • policy · public policy / implement a policy
    • The policy aims to reduce waste without increasing household costs.

主动回忆

  1. 遮住中文，口头解释每个词。
  2. 从中选 3 个词，各写一句与 IELTS 常见议题有关的句子。
  3. 检查词形和搭配，不追求堆砌生僻词。
展开英文释义
  • analyse — to examine something carefully in order to understand it
  • approach — a way of dealing with a problem or task
  • available — able to be obtained, used, or reached
  • benefit — an advantage or a helpful effect
  • concept — an abstract idea used to understand or organise something
  • data — facts or measurements collected for analysis
  • estimate — a calculation or judgment based on incomplete information
  • factor — one of the causes or conditions that influences a result
  • interpret — to explain the meaning of information or understand it in a particular way
  • legislate — to make or introduce laws
  • method — a planned way of doing or studying something
  • occur — to happen or exist in a particular situation
  • percent — one part in every hundred
  • period — a length or stage of time
  • policy — a plan or principle adopted by an organisation or government
