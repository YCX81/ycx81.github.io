🔧 嵌入式知识：FOC 磁场定向控制深入篇（Round 2）

1. SVPWM 空间矢量调制详解

为什么 SVPWM 优于 SPWM：
SPWM（正弦 PWM）独立调制三相占空比，直流母线利用率仅 86.6%。SVPWM 把三相电压视为一个空间矢量，用相邻基本矢量合成目标矢量，母线利用率提升至 100%（线性调制区）。

关键细节：

  • 七段式 vs 五段式：七段式（0127…7210）波形对称、谐波小，但开关次数多；五段式减少开关损耗，适合高功率场景
  • 死区补偿：deadtime 导致实际输出电压偏离指令值，尤其低速时影响显著，需根据电流方向补偿

3. MTPA 与弱磁控制

MTPA（Maximum Torque Per Ampere）：
对于 IPMSM（Ld ≠ Lq），纯 Iq 控制并非最优。注入适当负 Id 利用磁阻转矩，相同电流下产生更大转矩：

弱磁控制（Field Weakening）：
转速超过额定点后，反电动势逼近母线电压上限。通过注入更多负 Id 削弱磁通，换取更高转速，但最大转矩随之下降（恒功率区）。

面试常见问法

Q1：SVPWM 七段式调制的原理是什么？
A：将调制周期分为七段，零矢量对称分配在首尾和中间，两个有效矢量按正弦规律分配作用时间。对称结构使输出波形谐波最小化，等效开关频率翻倍。

Q2：无感 FOC 低速性能差的根本原因？
A：反电动势幅值正比于转速，低速时 BEMF 信号微弱，信噪比极低，观测器无法可靠估算转子位置。解决方案：高频注入法（需要凸极电机）或启动阶段用 I/f 开环过渡。

Q3：MTPA 控制的物理意义？
A：在电流矢量轨迹上找到使 T/|I| 最大的工作点。对于 IPMSM，因 Ld < Lq，注入负 Id 可同时获得永磁转矩和磁阻转矩，同等转矩下铜耗最小，效率最高。

Q4：弱磁控制为什么存在失控风险？
A：弱磁状态下 Id 为大幅负值，若控制器突然失效（如过流保护跳闸），Id 归零，磁通恢复到额定值，高转速下反电动势瞬间远超母线电压，产生不可控的再生电流，可能损坏功率器件。需要安全策略：逐步退出弱磁 + 泄放电阻。

逐词详解

  1. commutate /ˈkɒmjʊteɪt/ — 换相 技术

    The inverter commutates the motor phases in the correct sequence to produce smooth rotation.

  2. salient /ˈseɪliənt/ — 凸极的；显著的 技术

    Salient-pole machines exhibit significant inductance variation between d and q axes.

  3. torque /tɔːrk/ — 扭矩，转矩 技术

    FOC decouples torque and flux by independently controlling I-q and I-d.

  4. flux /flʌks/ — 磁通，通量 技术

    The d-axis current component directly controls the rotor flux linkage.

  5. sinusoidal /ˌsaɪnəˈsɔɪdəl/ — 正弦的 技术

    FOC produces sinusoidal phase currents, resulting in lower torque ripple than trapezoidal drive.

  6. modulation /ˌmɒdjʊˈleɪʃən/ — 调制 技术

    Space-vector modulation achieves higher bus utilization than carrier-based sinusoidal modulation.

  7. inverter /ɪnˈvɜːrtər/ — 逆变器 技术

    A three-phase inverter uses six switches arranged in three half-bridge legs.

  8. attenuate /əˈtɛnjʊeɪt/ — 衰减，减弱 技术

    The low-pass filter attenuates switching noise from the current measurement.

  9. stator /ˈsteɪtər/ — 定子 技术

    The stator windings carry the phase currents that create the rotating magnetic field.

  10. rotor /ˈroʊtər/ — 转子 技术

    The rotor permanent magnets provide the excitation flux in a PMSM.

  11. inductance /ɪnˈdʌktəns/ — 电感 技术

    The q-axis inductance determines how quickly the torque-producing current can change.

  12. impedance /ɪmˈpiːdəns/ — 阻抗 技术

    At high electrical frequencies, the winding impedance limits achievable current.

  13. decouple /diːˈkʌpəl/ — 解耦 技术

    FOC decouples the d and q axis dynamics using feedforward compensation terms.

  14. feedforward /ˈfiːdˌfɔːrwərd/ — 前馈 技术

    Adding a feedforward term for back-EMF improves transient response in the current loop.

  15. transient /ˈtrænziənt/ — 瞬态的 技术

    The controller must handle transient load steps without losing synchronization.

  16. bandwidth /ˈbændwɪdθ/ — 带宽 技术

    A current-loop bandwidth of 1 kilohertz is typical for industrial servo drives.

  17. overshoot /ˌoʊvərˈʃuːt/ — 超调 技术

    Excessive PI gain causes current overshoot that may trigger the overcurrent protection.

  18. saturation /ˌsætʃəˈreɪʃən/ — 饱和 技术

    Magnetic saturation in the iron core reduces effective inductance at high currents.

  19. ripple /ˈrɪpəl/ — 纹波 技术

    Current ripple amplitude is inversely proportional to PWM frequency and inductance.

  20. harmonics /hɑːrˈmɒnɪks/ — 谐波 技术

    Fifth and seventh harmonics in the phase currents produce sixth-order torque pulsation.

  21. resolver /rɪˈzɒlvər/ — 旋转变压器 技术

    A resolver provides absolute position feedback with high noise immunity in harsh environments.

  22. encoder /ɪnˈkoʊdər/ — 编码器 技术

    An incremental encoder outputs A/B quadrature pulses and an index signal per revolution.

  23. quadrature /ˈkwɒdrətʃər/ — 正交的 技术

    The q-axis is in quadrature with the d-axis, leading it by 90 electrical degrees.

  24. interpolate /ɪnˈtɜːrpəleɪt/ — 插值 技术

    The observer interpolates rotor angle between encoder edges for smoother commutation.

  25. discretize /dɪˈskriːtaɪz/ — 离散化 技术

    The continuous PI controller must be discretized using bilinear or forward-Euler methods.

  26. integral /ˈɪntɪɡrəl/ — 积分 技术

    The integral term in the PI controller eliminates steady-state current error.

  27. proportional /prəˈpɔːrʃənəl/ — 比例的 技术

    The proportional gain sets the initial stiffness of the current response.

  28. windup /ˈwaɪndʌp/ — 积分饱和 技术

    Anti-windup logic clamps the integrator when the output hits the voltage limit.

  29. clamp /klæmp/ — 钳位，限幅 技术

    Clamp the d-q voltage vector magnitude to stay within the inscribed circle.

  30. magnitude /ˈmæɡnɪtjuːd/ — 幅值，大小 技术

    The magnitude of the reference vector determines the modulation index.

  31. phasor /ˈfeɪzɔːr/ — 相量 技术

    The current phasor angle relative to the back-EMF determines the power factor.

  32. reluctance /rɪˈlʌktəns/ — 磁阻 技术

    Interior PM motors exploit reluctance torque in addition to magnet torque.

  33. cogging /ˈkɒɡɪŋ/ — 齿槽效应 技术

    Cogging torque causes vibration at low speed and is mitigated by skewing the stator slots.

  34. skew /skjuː/ — 斜置，倾斜 技术

    Skewing the rotor magnets by one slot pitch reduces cogging torque significantly.

  35. bemf /biːˌiːˌɛmˈɛf/ — 反电动势 技术

    The BEMF waveform shape reveals whether the motor is suitable for sinusoidal or trapezoidal drive.

  36. deadtime /ˈdɛdtaɪm/ — 死区时间 技术

    Deadtime insertion prevents shoot-through but introduces voltage distortion at low speeds.

  37. shoot-through /ˈʃuːtθruː/ — 直通（上下桥臂同时导通） 技术

    Without deadtime, both high-side and low-side switches conducting simultaneously cause shoot-through.

  38. bootstrap /ˈbuːtˌstræp/ — 自举 技术

    A bootstrap capacitor charges during the low-side on-time to drive the high-side gate.

  39. dissipate /ˈdɪsɪpeɪt/ — 耗散 技术

    Each switching event dissipates energy in the MOSFET during the transition period.

  40. thermal /ˈθɜːrməl/ — 热的，热学的 技术

    Thermal derating limits continuous current when the heatsink temperature exceeds 85 degrees Celsius.

  41. derate /diːˈreɪt/ — 降额 技术

    The drive derates maximum torque when junction temperature approaches the thermal limit.

  42. regenerative /rɪˈdʒɛnərətɪv/ — 再生的 技术

    During regenerative braking, kinetic energy flows back to the DC bus through the inverter.

  43. decrement /ˈdɛkrɪmənt/ — 递减 技术

    The speed ramp decrements the reference by a fixed step each control period.

  44. slew /sluː/ — 摆率，转换速率 技术

    Limiting the voltage slew rate reduces EMI radiated from the motor cables.

  45. notch /nɒtʃ/ — 陷波 技术

    A notch filter at the mechanical resonance frequency prevents oscillation in the speed loop.

  46. damping /ˈdæmpɪŋ/ — 阻尼 技术

    Insufficient damping in the speed loop causes sustained oscillation around the setpoint.

  47. setpoint /ˈsɛtˌpɔɪnt/ — 设定值，目标值 技术

    The speed setpoint ramps from zero to target over 500 milliseconds to limit inrush current.

  48. inertia /ɪˈnɜːrʃə/ — 惯量，惯性 技术

    High rotor inertia requires a more aggressive speed-loop integral gain for fast settling.

  49. backlash /ˈbæklæʃ/ — 齿隙，回差 技术

    Gear backlash introduces a dead zone that degrades position-loop accuracy.

  50. dither /ˈdɪðər/ — 抖动 技术

    Injecting dither into the PWM duty cycle improves ADC effective resolution at low currents.

💬 地道表达

1. “get the ball rolling”

意思： 启动，开始行动
场景： 项目启动或推动停滞事务时，号召大家先迈出第一步

A: We’ve been planning the FOC migration for months but nothing’s happening.
B: Let’s get the ball rolling — start with the Clarke transform module and iterate from there.

引用：「The hardest part of any project is getting the ball rolling — once momentum builds, everything accelerates.」— Eric Ries, The Lean Startup (2011)

2. “turn the tables”

意思： 扭转局面
场景： 形容从劣势逆转为优势，或局面发生戏剧性反转

A: The motor was oscillating wildly at low speed — we nearly scrapped the whole design.
B: Adding the sliding-mode observer completely turned the tables — smooth as butter now.

引用：「Edmond Dantès spent fourteen years in prison before he turned the tables on every man who had wronged him.」— Alexandre Dumas, The Count of Monte Cristo (1844)

3. “a tall order”

意思： 艰巨的任务
场景： 形容某个要求或目标难度极高，实现起来挑战巨大

A: Can we get sensorless FOC running at zero speed with position accuracy under 5 degrees?
B: That’s a tall order — you basically need high-frequency injection and a very clean signal chain.

引用：「Achieving consistent profitability in a bear market is a tall order, even for seasoned fund managers.」— The Wall Street Journal (2015)

4. “back to square one”

意思： 回到原点，从头再来
场景： 之前的方案或进展被推翻，不得不重新开始

A: The PI gains we tuned yesterday don’t work with the new motor at all.
B: Back to square one — different inductance means a completely different plant model.

引用：来源于英国棋盘游戏 Snakes and Ladders 的比喻，踩到蛇滑回起点 (1940s)

5. “in full swing”

意思： 全面展开，正在高潮
场景： 形容某项活动、项目或计划已经进入最活跃、最忙碌的阶段

A: How’s the motor control project coming along?
B: In full swing — we’ve got torque control working and the speed loop is next on the list.

引用：「The holiday shopping season is now in full swing, with retailers reporting record online traffic.」— The New York Times (2018)
